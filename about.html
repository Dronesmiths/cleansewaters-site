Founded on Family Values & Coastal Care

Cleansing Waters started with one truck, a water tank, and a commitment to help neighbors protect their coastal investments.

Our Story

Born and raised in Daytona Beach, siblings Ava and Carter Reynolds combined their backgrounds in hospitality and marine maintenance to create Cleansing Waters. What began as a weekend car detailing venture quickly grew into a full-service cleaning company serving waterfront homes, resorts, and marinas.

Today our technicians undergo continuous training in eco-friendly methods, water conservation, and equipment safety. Every project—whether a driveway refresh or a resort pool contract—receives the same meticulous attention.

Licenses & Certifications

  • PWNA Soft Wash & Environmental Compliance
  • Auto Detailing Association Member
